Well this was on my 18th birthday. I always wanted to spend my birthday something differently and had this plan from a long time which finally was executed. The old age homewas situated some 10 to 11 kms from my home. My mom and I took biscuits and some fruits for them. Well since this was my first time I was excited as well as bit emotional. We took permission to meet them from their home warden and they took some id proofs and let us in. The environment there was a bit different then I expected. Though they kinda look happy but still u can feel their pain of not living with their family. It looked like a big joint family talking in small groups, I meet each and every person over there. Some were bed ridden, some were caring for them, some of person from them cutting vegetables for their meal, making tea, like doing there regular activities. Well the best part is when I visted them all of them were so happy may be because I reminded me of there grandchildren. They were so welcoming everyone wanted to talk and tell me about them, there stories, they also told me to never leave my parents whatever is the reason and to treat each one with respect.Few people among them were well educated who took care about the finance part may be.I felt very emotional seeing them but all in all it was one of my best birthday. I feel one must definitely visit these people to know what it feel if u treat your parents like this. Thank you
